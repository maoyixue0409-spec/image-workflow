# Style Index Example

- `clean-multi-photo-layout.jpg`: photo-first collage, natural background, low doodle density, handwritten English title. Good for travel and lifestyle diaries.
- `food-cutout-layout.jpg`: restaurant/cafe layout, food white-edge stickers, warm background, small hand-drawn marks. Good for food trips and cafe posts.
- `scrapbook-diary.jpg`: tape, paper labels, date stamps, soft colors. Good for personal diary posts.
- `retro-film-layout.jpg`: warmer tones, darker contrast, grain, typewriter-like captions. Good for nostalgic stories.
